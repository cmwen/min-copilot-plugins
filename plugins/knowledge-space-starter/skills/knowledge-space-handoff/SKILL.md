# Purpose

Use this skill to move work cleanly between product, research, architecture, and engineering without losing context.

# Workflow

1. Restate the objective and the definition of done.
2. Summarize the most important inputs received so far.
3. Identify decisions already made versus questions that are still open.
4. Translate the current state into next-role deliverables and validation expectations.
5. Highlight blockers, dependencies, and assumptions that must remain visible.

# Output format

Use this structure:

## Objective
- Desired outcome
- Done condition

## Confirmed inputs
- Requirements
- Constraints
- Research findings
- Architecture notes

## Open questions
- What still needs an answer

## Next-role deliverables
- What the receiving role should produce

## Validation plan
- How the next step should be checked

## Risks and dependencies
- What could block or reshape the work

# Quality bar

- Optimize for clarity and transferability.
- Keep unresolved items visible.
- Do not bury key assumptions in prose.
